# SchoolCampus

**SchoolCampus** is a complete, cloud-based school management solution designed to simplify and automate operations for educational institutions of all sizes. From student management and staff hiring to exams, fees, and communication, SchoolCampus brings everything into one powerful, easy-to-use platform.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🎓 **Student Management**  
  Register students, track attendance, manage grades, and monitor academic progress.

- 👩‍🏫 **Employee Management & Hiring**  
  Manage teacher and staff records, recruitment processes, roles, payroll, and more.

- 📝 **Exams & Results**  
  Schedule exams, manage grading, generate report cards, and share results with parents and students.

- 💰 **Fees & Payments**  
  Automate fee collection, send reminders, track due payments, and generate detailed reports.

- 🔔 **Notifications & Communication**  
  Built-in messaging system for communicating with students, parents, and staff in real-time.

- ☁️ **Cloud-Based Platform**  
  Access SchoolCampus from anywhere at any time – all you need is a device with internet.

- 📊 **Analytics & Reporting**  
  Real-time insights and reports to support better decision-making and school performance tracking.
